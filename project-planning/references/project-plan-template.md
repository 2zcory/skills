# Project Plan Template

Use this as a compact creation template.

## Roadmap Question

What sequence matters most right now, and what mistake would be most expensive?

## Active Workstreams Or Current Phase

- name the real phase or the real streams

## Desired Outcomes

- 3-6 bullets

## Anti-goals

- what must not happen

## Known Facts

- what is already true

## Working Assumptions

- what is being assumed safely for now

## Constraints

- owner, time, platform, release, or scope limits

## Unknowns

- the open questions that still matter

## Slices

For each slice:

- Goal
- Why now
- Dependencies
- Dependency confidence
- Evidence quality
- Evidence needed
- Decision boundary
- Invalidation check
- Exit criteria
- Stop/defer notes

## Replan Triggers

- what would change the plan later

## What Not To Build Yet

- explicit deferrals

## Next Verification Need

- what must be reviewed or proven next

## Bottom Line

- the short governing conclusion
